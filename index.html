Call for Papers

We invite original, unpublished work on topics including zero-knowledge protocols and systems, post-quantum cryptography for decentralized networks, verifiable and privacy-preserving computation, decentralized identity (DID/VC), data availability and sampling, rollups and DA layers, MPC/threshold cryptography, CRDT-enabled P2P, and decentralized storage/compute markets.

Submissions should follow the host conference format and anonymization policy if applicable. Typical length: N pages excluding references; include an appendix for artifacts if needed.

At least one author of each accepted paper must register and present at the workshop.
